Privacy Policy

Applies to Best Inspirational Quotes and Цитата Дня.

We respect your privacy. This policy explains what information our apps do and do not collect. By using either app you agree to the practices described here.

Information we collect

Our apps do not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information. We do not require account registration, and we do not ask for your name, email address, phone number, or location.

Preferences such as your favorite quotes and app settings are stored locally on your device. This data never leaves your device and is removed when you delete the app.

Third-party services

Apple may collect anonymous, aggregated diagnostic and usage data through the App Store and iOS, in accordance with Apple's Privacy Policy. We do not have access to information that identifies you personally.

Children's privacy

Our apps do not knowingly collect any information from children. The apps are suitable for general audiences.

Support

We're here to help with both apps.

For questions, bug reports, feedback, or feature requests for either Best Inspirational Quotes or Цитата Дня, please reach out by email. We typically respond within 2–3 business days.

Email: contact@toonigo.com

Before contacting support

  • Make sure your iOS version and the app are up to date.
  • Try restarting the app, then your device.
  • Reinstalling the app resolves most issues (note: this clears local favorites).

When reporting a bug, please include

  • Which app you're using (Best Inspirational Quotes or Цитата Дня).
  • App version and iOS version.
  • Device model (e.g., iPhone 15).
  • Steps to reproduce the issue.
